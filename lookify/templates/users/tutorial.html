How Do You Use Lookify?

Tutorial


Creating An Account

If you don't have an account already, click the register button located on the top right corner of the page. From there, fill in the information and be sure to remember your username and password.

Setting Up Your Account

Let's familiarize ourselves with the layout. First you can access a dropdown menu when you click your username in the top right corner of the page. Here you can see how others view your profile with the "See Profile" page, manage all your looks and profile information through the "Dashboard", and logout. The round circle besides your username is your profile picture, which can be changed as often as you like. To the left of that is an easy access button to posting an opportunity of your own, but we'll get into that later.

Navigating The Dashboard

Let's check out the Dashboard first. Here you can edit your profile and see your inbox where people can sent private messages to you. On the left sidebar, you can navigate to these pages plus creating an opportunity of your own, managing it and checking up on the status of all the opportunities you've applied to. You can always go back to the homepage by clicking the Lookify logo on the top left corner.

Editing Your Profile

On the "Edit Profile" page you can enter any information you want to share with others as well as upload pictures representing you. The boxes indicating you to upload something, will allow you to use any file on your computer to be displayed on your profile once clicked. Clicking the blue text stating "Current ..." will show you what image is being currently displayed. You don't have to upload an image if you do not wish.

Experience & Education

Using the two buttons at the top of the "Edit Profile" form, you can add an relevant experience and education that you want others to see. Every listing you make appears at the top with all the details displayed. You can delete these by clicking the trash can icon.

Making A New Look

A "Look" is essentially a job/opportunity posting. Here you'll need to fill out some information regarding what your opportunity is and who should apply for it. The format is essentially the same as the editing profile page. You can easily come to this page by clicking the "Post Look" button seen on the homepage beside the username.

Managing Your Looks

On the "Manage Looks" page, you can track how many applicants have applied to the opportunities you've put out. Here you can also opt to delete an opportunity by clicking the trash can icon.

Tracking Your Applications
(Individual Accounts Only)

Let's say you've applied to an opportunity already. Here, you can look through your applications for different opportunities and see your status on each of them. If you can't find an application, then its likely that opportunity post was already deleted.

Checking For Private Messages

On the "Inbox" page, you'll see all the people wanting to reach out or contact you personally and not for a specific opportunity. It's essentially just a way for you to see who's reaching out and how they want to communicate with you.

Your Profile & How To DM Others

In the dropdown menu accessed by clicking your username, you'll see a button called "See Profile". Here you'll be able to see how your information is presented to others. This is also the same format in which you can see other users' information. An extra addition to this page is the contact feature, in which you can contact that person by filling out the form displayed on the right side of the profile.

Finding Organizations

Opportunities aren't the only thing you can find here. Lookify enables you to discover organizations that are looking for a plethora of candidates for many different positions. By clicking the "Find Organizations" page, you can search for a company by their name or keyword. If there's nothing in particular you want to find, you can browse by scrolling.

Finding Opportunities

Now that you have an account, you can access the "Find Opportunities" and "Find Organizations" pages. Let's start with the "Find Opportunities" which shows you current opportunities in the market. You can filter through tags, location and duration. By clicking the details button beside each opportunity, you can learn more about what they're looking for and what they offer.

Applying To Opportunities
(Individual Accounts Only)

Now that you've figured out how to navigate the site and understand all the features we offer, let's answer the most important question of all. You can apply to any opportunity by first clicking the details button of the "Look" card. When you see a page with all the information about the available postion, on the right should be a button called, "Apply Now". From there you only have to fill out a simple form and you're done!


And You're Done!


Enjoy creating opportunities of your own or applying to them. We wish you the best of luck!
